Chapter 1: Introduction to Computers

Comprehensive Notes, Questions, and Practice for Sindh Textbook Board

Chapter Notes

This chapter introduces the basics of computers, their history, components, and applications.

What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic device that processes data according to a set of instructions (program) to produce information.

History of Computers

  • Abacus: Ancient calculating tool.
  • Pascaline: Mechanical calculator by Blaise Pascal.
  • ENIAC: First electronic computer (1945).
  • Modern Computers: PCs, laptops, smartphones.

Components of a Computer

  • Hardware: Physical parts (CPU, monitor, keyboard).
  • Software: Programs and data (OS, applications).
  • Data: Raw facts; Information: Processed data.

Types of Computers

  • Analog: Measures continuous data (e.g., thermometer).
  • Digital: Processes discrete data (e.g., PCs).
  • Hybrid: Combination of both.

Generations of Computers

  • 1st Generation: Vacuum tubes (1940s).
  • 2nd Generation: Transistors (1950s).
  • 3rd Generation: Integrated circuits (1960s).
  • 4th Generation: Microprocessors (1970s).
  • 5th Generation: AI and parallel processing (1980s onwards).

Applications

Education, business, entertainment, communication, etc.
